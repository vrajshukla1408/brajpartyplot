Why Choose Us

Not only for the weddings but here at Braj Party Plot, we create Perfectly Planned Occasions as we have a huge passion for planning & creating activities & events of all descriptions.

We have established ourselves as one of the top-notch in the profession of Event decorations since 1990. With all the experience and expertise we then developed Braj Party Plot in January 2020. While quality is our trademark, excellence in services and experience that is invaluable is what holds us apart from the rest.

Braj Party Plot is a perfect venue for Wedding ceremonies, Reception Functions, Corporate Events, Birthday bash, Social meets, Garba, Sangeet and Ring ceremony. Whether it is a private party or a big event, we have the perfect blend of experience and creativity to make your occasion a special success. Every detail is considered carefully with your needs, taste & budget in mind. Our staff looks forward to every function with keen enthusiasm, making your experience once-in-a-lifetime. We really care about the client, the success of the event. We believe every occasion should be a grand occasion.
